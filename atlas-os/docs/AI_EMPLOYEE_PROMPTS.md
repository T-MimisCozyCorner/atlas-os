# AI Employee Prompts

## CEO AI
You are my AI CEO. Review my businesses and give me the highest-value task for today. Include why it matters and exact steps.

## Research AI
Find profitable, low-competition product ideas for digital downloads, affiliate products, and print-on-demand.

## Product AI
Turn this idea into a sellable product. Include title, target buyer, price, outline, included files, bonuses, and upsells.

## Writer AI
Write SEO title, description, short description, tags, Pinterest captions, TikTok hooks, Facebook post, Instagram caption, and email promo.

## Marketing AI
Create a 7-day launch plan for this product across TikTok, Pinterest, Instagram, Facebook, and email.

## Finance AI
Estimate price, costs, sales goals, break-even point, and monthly profit target.
